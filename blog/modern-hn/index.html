Modern HN: Because It’s Not 2004 Anymore

Hacker News is arguably the best aggregator of tech news on the planet, but let's be blunt: its interface is violently orange and looks like it was designed in the late '90s. Modern HN fixes this travesty.

It beautifies the brutalist interface, introduces modern readability, adds infinite scrolling, and generally makes parsing through intense technical arguments about JavaScript frameworks much easier on your eyes.
