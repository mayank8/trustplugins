Painty: Scribble All Over the Web

Sometimes you just need to explicitly draw a giant red circle on a web page to prove a point. Painty provides a minimalistic floating toolbar that lets you draw, highlight, erase, and annotate directly on top of live website content.

It’s phenomenal for presentations, giving design feedback, or just satisfying the inner child who occasionally wants to draw moustaches on corporate headshots before sending a screenshot.
